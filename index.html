Understanding Early Childhood Contexts

Early childhood development is deeply influenced by the environments in which children grow, learn, and interact. Factors such as economic conditions, family structure, cultural background, health, and life experiences shape children's wellbeing, learning outcomes, and overall development. Recognising these contexts is essential for creating inclusive and responsive early childhood education practices.
